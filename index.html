FAQ

Good questions, straight answers.

How long does setup take?+
Most teams are connected in under five minutes. You authorise your sources, Lumen models them automatically, and you can ask your first question right away.
Do I need to know SQL or formulas?+
No. That's the whole point. You ask in plain English and Lumen handles the querying, joining, and math behind the scenes.
Is my data safe?+
Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, access is scoped per user, and it's never used to train shared models. Your numbers stay yours.
Which tools does Lumen connect to?+
Google Ads, GA4, Google Search Console, most major CRMs, Google Sheets, and BigQuery today — with more added every month.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. There are no lock-in contracts on monthly plans — cancel in one click and you keep access through the end of your billing period.
